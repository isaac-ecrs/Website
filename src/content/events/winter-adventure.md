## What to Bring

- Comfortable clothing for activities and relaxation
- Layers for varying indoor/outdoor temperatures
- Outdoor gear for winter activities (sledding, hiking)
- Any special dietary items if needed
- Musical instruments if you'd like to join in!

## Local Attractions

During free time, explore the beautiful Finger Lakes region:

- **Corning Museum of Glass**: World-renowned collection and live glassmaking demonstrations
- **Winter Activities**: Sledding hills, hiking trails, and beautiful winter scenery on 600 acres
- **Downtown Corning**: Shops, restaurants, and the historic Gaffer District
